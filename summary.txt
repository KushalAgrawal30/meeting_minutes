FinTech Plus SYNC's Q2 2023 earnings call revealed a successful quarter with revenue reaching $125 million, a 25% year-over-year increase. The gross profit margin was 58%, with EBITDA surging to $37.5 million (30% margin). Net income rose to $16 million, up from $10 million in Q2 2022. The company expanded its total addressable market through new product lines and diversified its asset-backed securities portfolio. Total assets reached $1.5 billion, with a solid equity base of $600 million and a debt-to-equity ratio of 1.5. Customer acquisition cost decreased by 15%, while lifetime value grew by 25%. Risk management includes a value at risk model. The forecast for the coming quarter is positive, with expected revenue of around $135 million. The upcoming IPO of Pay Plus is expected to raise $200 million.
