FinTech Plus SYNC reported a stellar Q2 2023 with revenue of $125 million, a 25% year-over-year increase. Gross profit margin was 58%, and EBITDA surged to $37.5 million (30% margin). Net income rose to $16 million. The total addressable market has grown, driven by new products. Investments include collateralized debt obligations, residential mortgage-backed securities, and AAA-rated corporate bonds. Total assets reached $1.5 billion, with a $600 million equity base and a debt-to-equity ratio of 1.5. Customer acquisition cost dropped by 15%, with lifetime value growing by 25%. The company uses a value at risk model and has a healthy tier-run capital ratio of 12.5%. Q3 revenue is expected to be around $135 million, driven by blockchain solutions and AI analytics. The upcoming Pay Plus IPO is expected to raise $200 million.
