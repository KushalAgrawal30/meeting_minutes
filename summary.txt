FinTech Plus SYNC's Q2 2023 earnings call revealed strong financial performance, with revenue reaching $125 million, a 25% year-over-year increase. Gross profit margin was a solid 58%, and EBITDA surged to $37.5 million (30% margin). Net income rose to $16 million, up from $10 million in Q2 2022. The company expanded its total addressable market with new product lines and diversified its asset-backed securities portfolio. Total assets reached $1.5 billion against $900 million in liabilities, resulting in an equity base of $600 million. User growth remained strong, with customer acquisition cost decreasing and lifetime value increasing. Risk management includes a value at risk model with a 99% confidence level. The forecast for the next quarter is positive, expecting revenue of around $135 million, driven by blockchain solutions and AI-driven predictive analytics. The upcoming IPO of Pay Plus is expected to raise $200 million.
