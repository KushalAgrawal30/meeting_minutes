FinTech Plus SYNC reported a strong Q2 2023 with revenue of $125 million, a 25% year-over-year increase. Gross profit margin was 58%, and EBITDA surged to $37.5 million (30% margin). Net income rose to $16 million. The company expanded its total addressable market through new product lines and diversified its asset-backed securities portfolio. Total assets reached $1.5 billion, with a debt-to-equity ratio of 1.5. The company saw substantial organic user growth, with customer acquisition cost dropping and lifetime value growing. They have a VAR model for risk management, expect revenue of around $135 million next quarter, and anticipate an IPO of their subsidiary Pay Plus to raise $200 million.
